Could it be astronaut cats?

Cats are fascinating animals and surrounded by mysteries. In independent and mysterious temperament, these felines are known for its agility, elegance and behavior that is often unpredictable. However, recently, a theory has gained ground on the internet and among conspiracy theorists: it would be cats astronauts?
The theory suggests that cats are not land animals, but rather extraterrestrial beings sent to carry out a specific mission on our planet. There are historical reports that point to the fact that that cats were considered sacred beings in different cultures and even associated with divine figures. This could indicate that cats have a much more mystical origin than previously thought previously.

In addition, there are theorists who believe that felines have telepathic abilities and are able to communicate with others alien species. This belief is based on reports from people who claim to have seen cats reacting to stimuli that are not perceptible to human beings, or even behaving in unusual way during astronomical events such as an aurora boreal. These and other indications were interpreted as signs of that cats would be interplanetary beings.
